  How to use this website
 

This website was created for those who would like to know more about people’s sleep. You are able to compare your sleep to others by inputting information about your sleep on the ‘Home’ page or if you’d just like to see how the time people went to bed and woke up varies from day to day, you can see ‘Weekly Statistics’.
